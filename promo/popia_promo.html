POPIA Is Not Optional.
It Applies to Every Business.

POPIA · Protection of Personal Information Act
From the moment you collect a client's name or email address, POPIA applies. Every sole trader, SME and corporation must comply — there is no minimum size threshold.
PAIA · Section 51 — Promotion of Access to Information Act
Every business in South Africa — regardless of size, industry or structure — must have a PAIA Manual in place. This is a standalone statutory obligation, separately enforceable.
