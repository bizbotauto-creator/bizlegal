Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need labour law services even if I only have a few employees?
Yes. The Basic Conditions of Employment Act, Labour Relations Act and National Minimum Wage Act apply to all employers, regardless of size. Even a single employee can refer a dispute to the CCMA — and if you're not prepared, it can be costly.
What makes BizLegal different from other HR consultancies?
Our advisors are admitted attorneys and practicing legal professionals — not HR generalists. This means we can represent you at the CCMA, draft legally sound documents, and give you advice that stands up in court.
Can I cancel the retainer if I no longer need the service?
Yes. Either party may terminate the agreement by giving 2 calendar months' written notice. There is no long-term lock-in — we earn your business every month.
What happens if I get an unexpected labour inspector visit?
Call us immediately. We'll guide you through the inspection process, advise you on your rights and obligations, and help you respond to any compliance notice issued. This is exactly why having a retainer in place before an inspection happens matters.
How much does it cost per month?
Our Labour Essentials package starts from R500 per month plus R150 per employee (excl. VAT). The exact amount depends on your team size and the level of cover you need. We'll give you a precise quote after a free consultation.
What area do you serve?
We have offices in Hermanus (Overberg), Vredendal (Weskus) and Cape Town. We serve businesses across the Overberg, Helderberg, West Coast and broader Cape Town regions. Telephonic and email advice is available nationwide.
