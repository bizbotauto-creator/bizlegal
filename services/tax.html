What’s Included

Comprehensive Tax & Accounting Services

Our partnered accounting practices cover the full range of tax, payroll and statutory obligations for South African businesses of all sizes.

Bookkeeping & Annual Financial Statements

Full bookkeeping services including preparation of annual financial statements in compliance with applicable accounting standards.

Tax Advice & SARS Returns

Income tax advice, preparation and submission of SARS tax returns, and assistance with tax exits and SARS-related queries across all tax types.

Payroll Administration

End-to-end payroll administration including monthly payslips, payroll reconciliations and all payroll-related statutory submissions.

VAT, PAYE, UIF, EMP501 & CIPC

Registration, calculation and submission of VAT returns, PAYE, UIF contributions, EMP501 reconciliations and all CIPC annual returns.

Company Registrations & CIPC Queries

New company registrations, name changes, director amendments and all other CIPC queries and submissions managed on your behalf.

Clearance Certificates

Tax clearance certificates for compliance, foreign transactions and immigration purposes — applied for and managed through SARS and the relevant authorities.

UIF Returns & Claims

Monthly UIF return submissions and assistance with UIF claims processes for employees and employers through the Department of Employment and Labour.

Statutory De-Registrations

Deregistration of companies, VAT numbers, PAYE references and other statutory registrations managed correctly and timeously with all relevant authorities.

Import & Export Licences

Application and management of import and export licences through the International Trade Administration Commission (ITAC) and SARS Customs.

How It Works

Structured. Deadline-Driven. Accurate.
