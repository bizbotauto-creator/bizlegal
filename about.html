About BizLegal

Practicing Legal Professionals. Not Just Consultants.

BizLegal was built on a simple belief: South African businesses — especially small and medium enterprises — deserve access to real legal expertise at an affordable, predictable cost.

Our Story

Built for Business Owners Who Need Real Solutions

BizLegal was founded with one purpose: to give South African SMEs access to the same quality of legal and compliance support that large corporates take for granted — without the large corporate price tag.

Too many small businesses are caught out by CCMA referrals, SARS penalties, POPIA enforcement notices or consumer complaints — not because they are bad employers or dishonest operators, but because they did not have the right advice at the right time. We exist to change that.

We operate on a monthly retainer model — no surprise invoices, no per-minute billing, no hesitation before picking up the phone. Your consultant knows your business, knows your industry, and is available when you need them.

With offices in Cape Town, Hermanus and Vredendal, we serve businesses across the greater Western Cape region — providing local accountability alongside national expertise.

What Drives Us

Our Principles

Every client engagement at BizLegal is shaped by the same commitments — to quality, to accessibility, and to long-term partnership.

One Consultant. Always.
You deal with the same dedicated consultant every time — no call centres, no re-explaining your business to a stranger, no generic advice.
Genuine Legal Expertise
Our services are led by admitted attorneys with High Court appearance rights — not generalist HR advisors. You get accurate, defensible legal advice.
Predictable Pricing
Fixed monthly retainers with no surprise invoices. You know exactly what you pay each month — and you get unlimited access to your consultant within your retainer.
Local. Rooted. Accountable.
We are not a Johannesburg firm with a Western Cape sub-office. We live and work in the same communities as our clients, and we are accountable to them directly.
We Grow With You
Our retainer model is designed for long-term partnership. As your business grows and your needs evolve, so does our service — without renegotiating from scratch.
Tailored, Never Generic
We invest time to understand your operations and your industry before giving advice. We do not distribute off-the-shelf templates and call it compliance.
